SWIMMING POOL

This swimming pool oasis offers an exceptional swimming experience with a variety of activities, including water aerobics, volleyball, lounging, and BBQs. The facility provides a serene ambiance with crystal clear waters, lush greenery, and towering palm trees. The sound of flowing water from waterfalls contributes to the tranquil atmosphere, making it a perfect place to escape from daily life. The pool is not just for swimming laps or floating, but also a place to socialize and meet new people. It's a tropical paradise in the heart of the city where one can relax and unwind.

Here are some of the guidlines:

1. Never leave children unattended: Adult supervision is essential at all times even children know how to swim.

2. Know your limits: Always be aware of your swimming abilities and avoid activities that exceed your skill level.

3. Don't run or dive: Running around the pool area can lead to slips and falls, which can result in serious injuries.

4. Use proper equipment: Use flotation devices, life jackets, and other safety equipment when necessary.

5. Keep the pool area clear: Keep the pool area clear of toys, furniture, and other items that can cause tripping hazards.
