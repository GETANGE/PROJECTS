ABOUT

Welcome to Mountain View Resort, a premier hotel located at the foot of Mt. Kenya.Our hotel management system ensures a seamless and pleasant stay for guests. Enjoy our fully-equipped gym and ample parking, while our experienced chefs cater to diverse dietary requirements. Our conference rooms can host both small and large business events with necessary equipment. We offer picturesque wedding occasion grounds with experienced event planners to make your special day unforgettable. Book hassle-free and spacious rooms for a comfortable stay. We prioritize excellent customer service and attention to detail to make your stay a memorable one.

SERVICES

PARKING SERVICES


Mountain View Resort provides hassle-free parking for all guests with ample space for cars and buses. Whether you are driving yourself or arriving in a groups, you can rest assured that your vehicle is safe and secure while you enjoy their stay.

SWIMMING POOL


Mountain View Resort provides you with a refreshing escape from the summer heat. With stunning views of the mountains and a poolside drinks spot, you can relax and enjoy a refreshing drink while soaking up the sun.

ROOMS


Mountain View Resort offers luxurious and spacious hotel rooms with comfortable beds, stylish decor, and modern amenities. Guests can relax and unwind in a comfortable and serene environment while enjoying stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

FOODS & DRINKS
